The 1000 Genomes data is available via [ftp](ftp://ftp.1000genomes.ebi.ac.uk/vol1/ftp/), [http](http://ftp.1000genomes.ebi.ac.uk/vol1/ftp/) and [Aspera](http://www.1000genomes.org/aspera). Any standard tool like wget or ftp should be able to download from our ftp or http mounted sites. [To use Aspera](http://www.1000genomes.org/faq/how-download-files-using-aspera) you need to download their client.
