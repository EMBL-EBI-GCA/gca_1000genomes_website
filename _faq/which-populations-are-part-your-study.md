These populations have been divided into 5 super populations

*   **AFR**, African
*   **AMR**, Ad Mixed American
*   **EAS**, East Asian
*   **EUR**, European
*   **SAS**, South Asian

When the code <strong>ALL</strong> is used this means that all individuals from that release are being considered.
