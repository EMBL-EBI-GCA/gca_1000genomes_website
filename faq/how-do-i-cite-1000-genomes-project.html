How do I cite the 1000 genomes project

  • citation
  • Data Access
Answer: 

When citing the 1000 Genomes project in general please use our most recent main paper which is currently An integrated map of genetic variation from 1,092 human genomes, McVean et Al, Nature 491, 56–65 (01 November 2012) doi:10.1038/nature11632. This paper is published under the creative commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 3.0 Unported licence, please feel free to share and redistribute the paper appropriately.
