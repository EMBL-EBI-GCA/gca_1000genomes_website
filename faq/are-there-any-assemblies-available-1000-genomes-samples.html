Are there any assemblies available for the 1000 genomes samples?

  • assembly
  • Data Access
Answer: 

Efforts to create de novo assemblies are under way within the project but no data is available for release. The Gerstein Lab at Yale University have created a diploid version of the NA12878 sequence. This is available from the Gerstein website under NA12878_diploid. When used groups should cite AlleleSeq: analysis of allele-specific expression and binding in a network framework, Rozowsky et al, Molecular Systems Biology 7:522
