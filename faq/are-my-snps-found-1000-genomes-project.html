Are my snps found by the 1000 genomes project

  • Data Access
  • tools
Answer: 

There are two ways to discover if your variants are found in our variant list.

The first is to use our Ensembl style browser which allows you to search both by rs number and by genomic location. Our tutorial gives more detailed instructions. 

The second is to use our Data Slicer which can also be accessed from the browser tools menu. This allows you to get specific regions of our VCF files. This can also be used in a command line manner.
