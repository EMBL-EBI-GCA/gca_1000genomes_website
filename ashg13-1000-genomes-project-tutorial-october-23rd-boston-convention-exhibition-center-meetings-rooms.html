#ASHG13 1000 Genomes Project Tutorial, October 23rd, Boston Convention & Exhibition Center, Meetings Rooms 156ABC

This meeting is free to attend but so we know how many people to expect please register at the Boston College registration page

Schedule

 6:30 p.m. Registration (outside room 156ABC)
 7:00 p.m. Description of the 1000 Genomes data, Erik Garrison, Boston College [pdf]
 7:18 p.m. How to access the data, Laura Clarke, European Molecular Biology Laboratory - European Bioinformatics Institute [pdf]
 7:36 p.m. Haplotype integration, Androniki Menelaou, University Medical Centre Utrecht [pdf]
 7:54 p.m. Population genetic analyses, Suyash Shringarpure, Stanford University [pdf]
 8:12 p.m. Structural variants, Bob Handsaker, The Broad Institute [pdf]
 8:30 p.m. How to use the data in disease studies, Elinor Karlsson, Harvard [pdf] Please note some figures have been withdrawn from these slides due to their pre publication status
 8:48 p.m. Q&A
 9:00 p.m. Adjourn

Location: Boston Convention & Exhibition Center(adjacent to The Westin Boston Waterfront) 415 Summer Street, Boston, MA
Meetings Rooms 156ABC
